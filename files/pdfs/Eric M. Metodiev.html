Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. Candidate in the Center for Theoretical Physics at MIT.

I am interested in the intersection of particle physics, mathematics, and machine learning. I work on problems motivated by the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) at CERN. My research uses insights from quantum field theory to develop new data-driven techniques for collider physics (and vice versa!).
